Project Case Study

Imbir i Ryż

Webiste fot restuarant which specialise in Asian cousine. Website shows the menu and has CTA to place quick order. Developed for desktop and mobile devices.

The challenge

We tried to develop webiste that simply presents the menu and responds to all possible customers' questions. We made sure that webiste is user-freindly and content is shown very clearly

Design from the scrach

We tried to be very clear and direct with practicality of the webiste. User want quickly look through menu and place the order. We stacked sections in a tested order to came up with best solution.

Version for mobile devices

We put strongly emphasis on mobile version of the webiste. We made sure webiste is well optimised. We also provided some changes in order to maintain excellent expiernece and functionality of the website.

Answer to all customer's questions

We prepared most common questions asked by cutomers. Sections show the offer and give an option and all necessary infomration to customer. Website also containes a CTA buttons to place order online or make a direct call to the restaurant.
